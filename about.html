ABOUT US

We are a project that aims to develop an online NFT museum built by the NFT community.
The museum works by having the several artworks locked and up for sale.

Uppon buying the NFT on OpenSea it becomes unlocked and is put on display on this website with the name of the NFT, the name of the NFT creator, and the name or alias of the NFT owner (the NFT owner can also make their name clickable and redirect to a page of their choice)Learn more.

For more information or if you wish to work with us and become a featured MOMA artist please contact museumofmetaart@gmail.com
